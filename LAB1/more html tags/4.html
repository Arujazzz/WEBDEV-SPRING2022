Easy Greek Peasant Salad

  • Difficulty low

  • 550 kcal for 1 serving
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 3 pieces
Goat cheese 150 grams
Red bell pepper 2 pieces
Olives 100 grams
Onion 1 piece
Cucumber 1 piece
Olive oilt 6 tbsp. spoon

Step 1: Peel and cut the tomatoes into cubes.

Step 2: Remove seeds from bell peppers and cut into strips.

Step 3: Peel the onion and cut into half rings.

Step 4: Peel the cucumber and cut into slices.

Step 5: Add MAGGI® SPICE BOUQUET and olive oil, mix with all vegetables.

Step 6: Cut the olives and cheese into cubes and add to the salad.

Tomato salad with garlic

  • Time: 15 min
  • Difficulty low
  • 2 servings
  • 313 kcal per serving
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 3 pieces
Garlic 3 cloves
Salt to taste
Mayonnaise 2 tbsp
Green onions 3 pieces
Cheeese 50 grams

Step 1: Rinse the tomatoes with water, cut into thin slices and put on the bottom of the salad bowl.

Step 2: Rinse the onion, finely chop and add to the tomatoes.

Step 3: Peel the garlic, grate it on a fine grater and put it on the onion. Then add salt and dress the salad with mayonnaise.

Step 4: Grate the cheese on a fine grater and sprinkle generously on top of the finished salad.

Cooking advice

This salad does not have to be prepared in layers, you can lay out the sliced in a chaotic manner, mix and serve in this form.
